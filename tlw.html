Puss in Boots: The Last Wish

About the Movie

Puss in Boots: The Last Wish was released in December 2022 and invites viewers on a high-stakes adventure to find the titular "Last Wish." As Puss discovers that he is down to the last life of his original nine, he is forced to confront his mortality.

Animation

The animation in this film was and is ground-breaking, as it expertly blends 3D animation with 2D textures. Though the majority of the film is 3D, every surface in the movie looks like watercolor paint. Viewers may notice that many highlights and details look like brushstrokes. Additionally, it alters the status quo of frame rate in animation. During many of the fight scenes, the animators chose to animate on twos. This decreased amount of frames effectively makes the audience feel as if the characters are moving even faster.

Social Implications

For obvious reasons, animation nerds lost their minds over the art style in this movie. It's so visually incredible that viewers can't help but love it. But more than that, this movie depicts mental health struggles, specifically anxiety and panic attacks in a very realistic and relatable way. It shows that mental health is something that affects everyone, but also how rapidly one's own mental health can change. Puss goes from "Fearless" and "Invincible" to feeling terrified and insignificant in a manner of hours in the timeline of the story. It's a beautiful story of growth and acceptance of one's own struggles.
