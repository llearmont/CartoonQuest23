About

This website summarizes and highlights the most noteworthy animated creations from the past twelve months. The cartoons featured on this site are all from different studios and creators, but they all have one thing in common: they are all pushing the boundaries of animation and storytelling. This is a subject every aspiring animator will want to keep an eye on so they can not only develop their own skills, but also gain inspiration for their own projects.
